Gareth Trelford - son, 07/10/2024

My father was let down and betrayed by those he should have been able to trust—his former partner and his solicitor—at his most vulnerable, as he faced the early symptoms of cancer and the stress of moving home. After his passing, my family and I experienced a profound sense of powerlessness, finding it impossible to carry out his wishes affectively, while dealing with personal attacks from his former partner. This made it impossible for us to grieve properly.

It is clear that when Aston contacted the solicitor to amend the will after an argument with his partner—discussing how to protect the estate from inheritance tax in the event of a future marriage—this was not intended to override the requirements he had carefully articulated just two months earlier. A subsequent investigation has confirmed this, as well as the expectations Aston communicated to his family, friends, myself and his former partner. He would tell me of these expectations many times in his final weeks "she will only be in the house for two years" and talk about how he wanted us, his children and grand children to benefit in our lives after this period. It is clear for all to see how my father would have felt if he had known how his former partner would make us suffer over these last 7 years

Navigating the challenges arising from this situation has been one of the most significant struggles of my life, deeply affecting my mental health. Fortunately, with the support of family and friends, I’ve managed to find my way through these difficult times, though not unscathed and carrying the ongoing uncertainty that surrounds this situation.

This issue extends beyond my personal experience; it impacts my father's entire family and friends. I am committed to dedicating my life to this cause and to restore my father’s honor. My aim is to campaign on these issues to help others who might also be affected.

When I share my father’s story with others, and the injustice becomes apparent, many express disbelief that it hasn’t been possible to resolve it in the way my father intended. I share their frustration and have to explain the complexities of the law in this scenario, the lack of legal protection from negligence and other factors. We are not alone in facing this completely unjust situation, and I am determined to campaign on these issues to help others affected and work toward meaningful change for the future.
